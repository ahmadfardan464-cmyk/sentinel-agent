## Recording Tips

**Tools:**
- OBS Studio (free screen recording)
- Audacity (voiceover recording, optional)
- CapCut/DaVinci Resolve (editing)

**Best Practices:**
1. Record terminal with good contrast (dark theme, large font)
2. Use smooth transitions between scenes
3. Add background music (lo-fi, non-distracting)
4. Keep pacing tight — 3 minutes max!
5. Export as MP4, 1080p, <50MB for easy upload

**Upload to:**
- YouTube (unlisted)
- Loom (quick share)
- Google Drive (direct link)
